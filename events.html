CCP

Career Counselling Program (CCP) was launched on 25th Jan with the prime aim to connect students of class 9-12th with college students who have outshined in their respective part and are motivated enough to transfer their experience to the building youth. The event saw a massive response of 70+ mentors mentoring one on one with 250+ mentees.

Objectives

  • Provide Career Guidance to budding youth in their desired stream
  • Connecting with mentors
  • Highlight factors affecting career decision
  • Mentor in making informed choices
  • Prepare for modern needs and requirements

Achievements of the Program

  • Around 250+ Mentees have been Mentored regarding the streams of their choice
  • Around 70+ mentors have been involved from prestigious colleges across the Country
  • Around 280+ Sessions have been done between Mentors and Mentees
  • 350+ hours of total Sessions.

RISE

RISE (Reform and Innovate School Education) initiative was started on 9th September 2020 till 5th October 2020 with an aim to improve the quality of education imparted in schools across the country. Itâ€™s overall mission is to transform the existing teacher-centric model into a student-centric model of education. Through our LiGHT centres we approached various schools throughout the nation for an online workshop.Ek Tara collaborated with us.

Aim of the Workshop

  • Facilitate improved learning for students of class 1-5th especially during the pandemic time and online learning
  • Provide teachers with the country the means of implementing activity-based learning lesson plans during pandemic and post-pandemic.
  • Promote student-centred teaching methods which ensure the holistic development of the students
  • Facilitate the implementations of the guidelines of the NEP (National Education Policy 2020)

Incentives of the Program.

  • Workshop Certificates were provided to both the teachers and the school.
  • Teachers learn new methods of teaching and learn how education becomes more interesting that ensures the overall development of the children.
  • Opportunity to become part of the nationwide social cause.

Achievements

  • 7 teachers conducted the activities in their school
  • 24 teachers participated in phase 2
  • 31 teachers participated in phase 1
  • 110 students participated in activities conducted
  • 8 cities with 15+ schools participated.

Cleanliness Drive

Cleanliness Drive was the first nationwide event conducted by 9 LiGHT Centers. It was conducted on 2nd October 2019 on the occasion of the birth anniversary of The Father of the Nation: Mahatma Gandhi and the launch of Swachh Bharat Abhiyaan. Cleanliness was the annual theme of LiGHT of the session 2019 - 2020 under which we conducted several cleanliness campaigns across the country.

Aim of the Drive

  • It aimed to instill the importance of cleanliness among the youth.
  • Facilitate the important facts and create awareness about cleanliness.
  • It aimed to mark the launch of the Swachh Bharat Mission from LiGHT.

Achievements of the Drive

  • 9 LiGHT Centers Participated in this Drive
  • 150+ volunteers from all over the LiGHT Centers participated in this Drive.
  • 11+ different areas in the region of LiGHT Centers were covered.
  • 20,000+ direct and indirect beneficiaries from the Drive.
