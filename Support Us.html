Every year LiGHT chooses an Annual Social Theme from one of the Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs) from the list of 8 SDGs which LiGHT has undertaken under it. For the academic session 2021-22, LiGHT has chosen “Quality Education” as an Annual Social Theme, and it has been doing so many activities under the theme.Under the Annual Social Theme: Quality Education, every LiGHT Center along with the Headquarters: LiGHT IIT Kharagpur plan their own activities and work for the betterment of their societies. The impact of the activities may vary from the nationwide to the local areas. Through these activities we try to create a positive change in as many beneficiaries as we can.

LIGHT

LIGHT is an initiative of one of India's largest student-run government registered NGO Gopali Youth Welfare Society, based in IIT Kharagpur. Gopali Youth Welfare Society aims at the overall socio-economic development of the underprivileged near the IIT Kharagpur campus.
